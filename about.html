YMusic

Hi, I'm Ice. I created this little youtube audio app when learning how to make Electron apps.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • focus search bar [/]
  • focus audio container [j]
  • play/pause audio [k]
  • loop/unloop audio [l]
  • mute/unmute audio [m]

Known issues

Audio from last session does not play on first load No idea why this happens, but it can be fixed by simply refreshing it once.
Unable to play flagged videos Sorry, Youtube does not allow us to access such videos. You can watch them on the official client, though. Also, the whole application might be rendered unusable, in that case please reload it again.
